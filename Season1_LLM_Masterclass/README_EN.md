> **"What I cannot create, I do not understand."** - Richard Feynman

This Masterclass is not just another tutorial that calls functions from the HuggingFace library.
It is a **10-step full-stack deep learning quest** that starts from the very bottom of language models (Word2Vec), moves through the heart of the Transformer (Attention), parameter optimization (LoRA), ethical alignment (RLHF), and reaches the pinnacle of autonomous Agent architecture. Every step is proven through **mathematical theory and raw PyTorch tensor code**.

## 🗺️ 10-Step Curriculum Roadmap

Each module is linked to an independent GitHub repository. Click the links to explore the `01_theory.md` and `02_algorithm_from_scratch.ipynb` of each stage!

### 🧱 Phase 1: Foundation (Mathematical Transformation of Language)
Learn how models understand and compress human language into numbers.
*   **Step 01. [Embeddings Basics](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/embeddings-basics)**
*   **Step 02. [Tokenization](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/tokenization)**

### 🏗️ Phase 2: Architecture (Evolution of Deep Learning Skeletons)
Trace the tensor history of overcoming sequential data processing limits and entering the Transformer era.
*   **Step 03. [RNN & Seq2Seq](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/rnn-seq2seq)**
*   **Step 04. [Attention Mechanism](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/attention-mechanism)**
*   **Step 05. [Transformer Architecture](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/transformer-architecture)**

### 🧠 Phase 3: Scaling & Optimization (Giant Models and Tuning)
Cover the architecture of the multi-billion parameter era and the optimization magic to train them on consumer GPUs.
*   **Step 06. [Pretrained Language Models](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/pretrained-language-models)**
*   **Step 07. [LLM Optimization (LoRA)](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/llm-optimization-lora)**

### 🤖 Phase 4: Production & Agents (Practical Applications and Autonomy)
Evolve LLMs from mere text generators into 'behaving systems that follow human ethics'.
*   **Step 08. [AI Alignment (RLHF & DPO)](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/llm-alignment-rlhf)**
*   **Step 09. [Advanced RAG (HNSW)](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/advanced-rag-hnsw)**
*   **Step 10. [Agentic Workflow & Tool Use](https://github.com/SuwonRockman/llm-agentic-workflow)**
